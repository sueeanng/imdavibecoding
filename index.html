What Is WoofWords?

WoofWords is a smart collar and app combo that decodes your dog’s barks and body language into emotions and needs. No more guessing if that bark means “play” or “please stop singing”.

📱

How It Works

Our collar records and analyses your dog's sounds and movements. The app translates them into real-time alerts like “I’m hungry!” or “I need a walk, human.”
